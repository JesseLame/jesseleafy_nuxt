title: Spicy Bean Sprout Salad
description: A quick and vibrant bean sprout side dish with sesame oil and spices.
description_long: |
  This spicy bean sprout salad is a simple yet flavorful side dish that brings together crunchy sprouts, nutty sesame oil, and a kick of seven-spice or five-spice seasoning. 
  It’s fast, fresh, and perfect for serving with rice or grilled mains. The dish is ready in minutes but delivers layers of taste — from umami-rich soy to aromatic spice and toasted sesame.
image: /images/spicy_bean_sprouts.jpg
ingredients:
  - 340 g bean sprouts
  - 2 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
  - 1.5 tsp soy sauce
  - 1.5 tsp shichimi togarashi (or Chinese five spice)
  - 0.5 tsp salt (or more to taste)
  - freshly ground black pepper
  - 1 Tbsp toasted white and black sesame seeds (for garnish)
  - chopped green onion or scallion (optional, for garnish)
instructions:
  - Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  - Add the bean sprouts and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  - Drain well to remove all excess water.
  - In a large bowl, combine sesame oil, soy sauce, spice mix, salt, and black pepper.
  - Add the cooked bean sprouts and mix thoroughly to coat.
  - Top with toasted sesame seeds and chopped green onion if desired.

# Spicy Bean Sprout Salad

A crunchy, spicy, and nutty bean sprout salad that's ready in under 10 minutes — a perfect accompaniment to rice bowls or grilled dishes.
