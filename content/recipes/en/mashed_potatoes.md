ingredients:
  - 1 kg potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  - 200 ml milk or cream, warmed
  - 1-2 tbsp mustard
  - 1 tbsp butter
  - salt, to taste
  - black pepper, to taste
instructions:
  - Place the potatoes in a pot and cover with cold salted water.
  - Bring to a boil and cook for 15-20 minutes, until fork-tender.
  - Drain well, then return the potatoes to the hot pot for 1-2 minutes to steam-dry.
  - Add the warm milk or cream, mustard, butter, and black pepper.
  - Mash until smooth and creamy. Add a splash more liquid if needed.
  - Taste and season with salt and extra pepper.

# Mustard Mashed Potatoes

A creamy mash with mustard and butter that pairs perfectly with hearty stews.
